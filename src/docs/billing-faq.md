# Billing & Plans FAQ

Frequently asked questions about ELIPHASx pricing, billing, and subscriptions.

---

## Plans & Pricing

### What plans are available?

| Plan | Price | Quotes/Month | Team Members | Features |
|------|-------|--------------|--------------|----------|
| **Trial** | Free (14 days) | 5 | 1 | Basic features |
| **Starter** | R299/mo | 50 | 3 | All features |
| **Professional** | R599/mo | Unlimited | 10 | Priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Custom branding |

### How does the free trial work?

- 14 days of full Professional features
- No credit card required to start
- Create up to 5 quotes
- All features unlocked

### What happens when my trial ends?

- You'll be prompted to choose a plan
- Your data is preserved for 30 days
- Downgrade to limited access without a subscription

## Billing

### When am I charged?

- Billing is monthly, on the date you subscribed
- Payments are processed automatically
- You'll receive an invoice via email

### What payment methods are accepted?

We use **Paystack** for secure payments:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Bank transfer (selected banks)
- USSD payments

### How do I update my payment method?

1. Go to **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. You'll be redirected to the Paystack portal
4. Update your card details

### Can I get a refund?

- Refunds available within 7 days of payment
- Contact support@basilx.co.za
- Partial refunds for unused days

## Upgrading & Downgrading

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm payment
5. New features available immediately

### What happens when I upgrade?

- Pay the difference for remaining days
- New limits apply immediately
- No data loss

### Can I downgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Takes effect at next billing cycle

> ⚠️ **Note:** Downgrading may limit your team size. Remove excess members first.

## Invoices & Receipts

### Where can I find my invoices?

1. Go to **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Scroll to **Billing History**
3. Click **Download** on any invoice

### What's included on the invoice?

- Company name and VAT number
- Plan details
- Payment amount and date
- Transaction reference

## Cancellation

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period ends

### What happens to my data?

- Data preserved for 30 days after cancellation
- Export your quotes before cancelling
- Contact support to request data deletion
