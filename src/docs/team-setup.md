# Team Setup Guide

Learn how to invite team members and manage roles in ELIPHASx.

---

## Understanding Roles

ELIPHASx has three user roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access, billing, team management |
| **Manager** | Approve quotes, view all quotes, manage clients |
| **Sales** | Create quotes, manage own clients |

---

## Inviting Team Members

### Step 1: Go to Team Settings

1. Click on your profile icon (top right)
2. Select **Team** from the dropdown
3. Click **+ Invite Member**

### Step 2: Fill in Details

- **Email:** The team member's work email
- **First Name:** Their first name
- **Last Name:** Their last name
- **Role:** Select from Admin, Manager, or Sales

### Step 3: Send Invitation

Click **Send Invite**. The team member will receive an email with:
- A welcome message
- A link to set their password
- Instructions to get started

---

## Managing Team Members

### Change a Role

1. Go to **Team** settings
2. Find the team member
3. Click the **Role** dropdown
4. Select the new role
5. Changes take effect immediately

### Remove a Team Member

1. Go to **Team** settings
2. Find the team member
3. Click the **⋮** menu (three dots)
4. Select **Remove from team**
5. Confirm the action

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Removed users lose access immediately but their quotes remain in the system.

---

## Plan Limits

Your team size depends on your subscription plan:

| Plan | Team Members |
|------|--------------|
| Trial | 1 user |
| Starter | 3 users |
| Professional | 10 users |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

To add more members, upgrade your plan in **Billing** settings.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Use Role-Based Access**
   - Assign Sales for quote creation
   - Assign Manager for approvals
   - Limit Admin access

2. **Require Quote Approvals**
   - Enable for high-value quotes
   - Managers review before sending

3. **Regular Audits**
   - Review team access quarterly
   - Remove inactive users
