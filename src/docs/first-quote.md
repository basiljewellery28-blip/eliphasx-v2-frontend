# Creating Your First Quote

Welcome to ELIPHASx! This guide will walk you through creating your first jewelry quote in just 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Add a Client

Before creating a quote, you need to add a client:

1. Go to **Clients** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Client**
3. Fill in the required information:
   - Client name
   - Company (optional)
   - Email
   - Phone
4. Click **Save**

---

## Step 2: Start a New Quote

1. Click **Create Quote** from the dashboard or sidebar
2. Select the client from the dropdown
3. Choose the piece category (Ring, Necklace, Bracelet, etc.)

---

## Step 3: Add Metal Details

Enter the metal specifications:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Metal Type | Gold, Silver, Platinum | 18K Yellow Gold |
| Weight | Grams of metal used | 5.5g |
| Spot Price | Auto-fetched or manual | R1,850/g |
| Wastage % | Typical 5-15% | 10% |
| Markup % | Your profit margin | 25% |

---

## Step 4: Add Production Costs

### CAD/Design
- Hours spent on CAD design
- Include any revisions
- Base rate is pre-configured

### Stones
- Stone type and quantity
- Cost per stone
- Setting labor

### Labor
- Manufacturing hours
- Finishing work
- Quality control

---

## Step 5: Review & Generate PDF

1. Check the **Totals** section at the bottom
2. Review all line items
3. Click **Download PDF** for:
   - **Client PDF** - Clean version for customers
   - **Admin PDF** - Full breakdown with margins
