## 5. How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted using TLS/SSL
- **Password Security:** Passwords hashed using bcrypt (12 rounds)
- **Access Control:** Role-based access to data
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute-force attacks
- **Account Lockout:** Automatic lockout after 5 failed login attempts
- **Regular Backups:** Daily encrypted backups

## 7. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate information
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information
4. **Object:** Object to processing of your information
5. **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing
6. **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
7. **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at eliphasxsupport@basilx.co.za
