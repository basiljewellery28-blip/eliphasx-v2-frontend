## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Required)

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security protection | Session |
| `preferences` | User interface settings | 1 year |

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be disabled.

---

## Cookies We Do NOT Use

- ❌ Advertising cookies
- ❌ Tracking cookies
- ❌ Third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics)
- ❌ Social media cookies

---

## Your Choices

Since we only use essential cookies, there is no opt-out mechanism as they are required for the Service to function.

If you disable cookies in your browser, some features may not work correctly.
